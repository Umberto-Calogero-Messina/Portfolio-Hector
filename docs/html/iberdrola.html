The ecosystem

Building the puzzle

We worked in their digital ecosystem while enhancing existing tools. Our work modernized their visual language, bringing consistency to the EV charging app, customer portal, and public website. The refreshed brand better communicates Iberdrola's sustainability values while maintaining all existing functionality - we improved usability without disrupting familiar workflows.

Customer App

We built a centralized hub where Iberdrola customers can access all their data and account information. We enhanced core functionalities while prioritizing accessibility and seamless integration with other digital assets – especially the Customer Portal.

Public Website

We crafted Iberdrola's digital flagship – a virtual showcase that brings their social and environmental commitment to life. Through a refined design system, we created an intuitive space where users can explore and sign up for services effortlessly.

Public Charging App

We developed an EV charging app for everyone – whether Iberdrola customers or not. It delivers a smooth, accessible experience for locating charging stations, with special attention to business users' needs.

My Customer Area

We upgraded Iberdrola's private customer portal with personalized UX improvements. The revamped interface offers clear, intuitive access to all account management features – including streamlined contract workflows for business clients.

Rebranding

Branding with Purpose

We led Iberdrola's rebranding initiative – from design system refinement to full implementation. Our focus: ensuring brand consistency across every customer touchpoint while optimizing design and development workflows for maximum impact.

Design System

The pillars of the experience

The existing system had grown organically, creating inconsistencies that impacted both user experience and team efficiency. We needed to create a new visual language that would reflect Iberdrola's innovative leadership and scale across all digital products

Owr Approach

We built the system on 4 foundational principles:

  • Optimistic: Energy with personality: Bright, hopeful colors and motion that make renewables feel exciting
  • Easy: Modular components with usability-first approach, accessible to all user types
  • Smart: Good design systems work smarter, not harder. How? By doing more with less.
  • Flexible: One system, infinite scales. Flexible patterns adapting from mobile apps to complex B2B dashboards

Results

Iberdrola keeps growing

The project proved how rebranding and UX must work hand-in-hand. By rebuilding both the visual identity and product ecosystem around our four principles, we achieved more than consistency, we created a recognizable digital experience. The key insight? True transformation happens when design systems and brand strategy evolve together."
